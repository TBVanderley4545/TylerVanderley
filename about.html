About

I'm Tyler Vanderley, but you can call me Ty. I am deeply interested in programming and the cool things that can be created with programming knowledge. I am a Mobile and Web Developer with experience in Java, XML, HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

I also love animals. This has led me to use my skillset to create an Android application for the Watauga Humane Society in Boone, NC, which is set for release at the end of May 2016 on the Google Play store.

I am "self-taught", but I have had great teachers to learn from. I will leave a mark on this world and create wonderful things for people to love for years to come. More updates in the works.
